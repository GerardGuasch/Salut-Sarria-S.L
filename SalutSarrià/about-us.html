About Us..

Who are we?

Welcome to our center

Salut Sarrià S.L.® is an Entity of the Associative Base (EBA) that manages the health care services Primary school in the health areas of Sarrià, Vallvidrera and Les Planes. Our is a model based on the participation and involvement of all professionals, health and non-health, with the aim of providing the best service to the community.

The result of this activity was the obtaining on October 21, 2015 of the accreditation as a Primary Care Team of Catalonia by the Department of Health, according to current regulations (Decree 86/2014 of 10 June ).

Mission

Resolve existing health needs in patients cared for Sarrià, Vallvidrera and Les Planes, respecting their privacy, autonomy, right to information and responsibility.

Vision

Being a firmly established company in the healthcare sector, with a projection of future, a benchmark in health, management, organizational and technological results, rated excellently by patients and very attractive by professionals.

Valors
  • Professionalism: Responsibility and commitment to work, working as a team and proudly belonging to the organization.
  • Results Orientation: Ability to seek the maximum benefit from patients with the efficient use of available resources.
  • Innovation and desire for improvement.
  • Respect for patients' rights.
